Reviewer: Claude Code  
Date: 2026-07-15  
Reviewed artifact: `authored/previews/ashford-authored-contact-sheet.png`

## Scope

The nine regenerated candidates were reviewed against
`AUTHORED-PACK-BRIEF.md`, approved `MV-HOM-ASH-001@0.1`, approved
`AVCP-HOM-ASH-001`, and the selected Ashford v2 concepts. This is visual and
canon-fidelity review only. It does not approve an assembly, prove runtime
behavior, or authorize production-map use.

Classification meanings:

- **Suitable for Chris selection:** no remaining visible canon or brief blocker;
  subjective acceptance still belongs to Chris.
- **Revision needed:** the current candidate fails a falsifiable brief item,
  but a materially different assembly may remain possible with the available
  vocabulary.
- **Impossible with current Outside vocabulary:** the candidate is only a
  proxy for a required noun and the available verified vocabulary does not
  safely express the requirement; do not iterate by relabeling the proxy.

## Building-family finding

The fix pass successfully lowers all four buildings and makes the Inn broadest,
so the previous excessive-height failure is closed. Role differentiation is
not yet closed: all four use one rectangular brown-roof shell, with function
communicated mainly through signs and loose props. That fails the approved
pairwise silhouette requirement in `ASH-BUILD-003` and the selected v2
building-family direction.

The current `Outside` vocabulary can still support different widths, offsets,
porches, attached volumes, and entrance treatments, so massing differentiation
is a revision task. The selected concept's exact dark-slate roof color is not
present in these candidates and should remain recorded as a vocabulary gap
rather than silently described as achieved.

## Infrastructure-proxy finding

Two proxies cross the canon boundary and should stop iterating within this
vocabulary:

- The roofed-well proxy is not visually a complete roofed well.
- The drainage proxy is explicitly a natural river bridge, the treatment the
  MapVision forbids as Ashford's drainage identity.

The warm vent is the only infrastructure candidate suitable for Chris's
selection pool. The panel and fence remain plausible authored targets after
substantial recomposition, but their current proxies do not pass.

## Reviewer verdict

- **Suitable for Chris selection:** 1 of 9 — Warm-Stone Vent.
- **Revision needed:** 6 of 9 — Elara House, Shop, Inn, Elder House, Humming
  Panel, Patched-Metal Fence.
- **Impossible with current Outside vocabulary:** 2 of 9 — Roofed Well,
  Maintenance Drainage.

No candidate is approved by this review. Do not use the two impossible proxies
in disposable Ashford generation as if they fulfilled canon; they require a
custom/expanded asset vocabulary or a separately approved change in visual
direction.
